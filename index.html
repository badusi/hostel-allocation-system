About Federal Polytechnic Ayede

Federal Polytechnic Ayede is committed to providing quality education and comfortable accommodation for all students. Our hostel allocation system ensures fair and transparent distribution of accommodation facilities.

We offer modern hostel facilities with essential amenities including WiFi connectivity, study areas, recreational facilities, and 24/7 security to ensure a conducive learning environment.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and secure accommodation for all students in a conducive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution in providing modern student accommodation facilities in Nigeria.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Student-centered service delivery in all our operations.
